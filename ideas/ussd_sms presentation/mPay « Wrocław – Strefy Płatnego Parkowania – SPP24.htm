mPay

mPay – choice no. 1 for your mobile payments

 

  • mPay stands for a convenient method of making financial transactions with your mobile phone
  • mPay was the first in Poland to obtain consent of the NBP President for settling payment transactions using mobile phone
  • mPay makes available to telecommunications carriers, banks, and other institutions a versatile platform for operating mobile payments

mPay – technology

  • The mPay system uses USSD and IVR technologies
  • USSD – functionality available for any mobile phone model
  • IVR – voice system allows to operate the service without having to look at your phone
  • Works without additional applications or exchange of a SIM card
  • The user is not charged with the cost of data transmission
  • Service is also available in areas where there is 3G network poor data coverage
  • Security – without additional costs for mPay System
  • Works basing on USSD communication channel, which is free for users. As a result the payment process does not generate additional costs arising from data transfer.
  • During the transaction sensitive user data are in no danger of being intercepted.
  • Integration with GSM operators guarantees security

More information can be found on the web page http://www.mpay.pl
